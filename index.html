Schedule Manager - BETA

ⓘ Quick Start Guide▶

This dashboard enables scheduling coordinators to efficiently build conflict-free course schedules, manage faculty and faculty assistants workloads, and perform automated compliance auditing.

GETTING STARTED:
1. Course Catalog – Download the sample CSV, preserve all 7 column headers exactly. Edit with as per your department's data, then upload.
2. Prerequisites/Co-requisites – Download the sample CSV, maintain the header structure. Define course dependencies to enable student view year-level recommendations.
3. Schedule – Download the sample schedule CSV, or load previously published semesters from the dropdown. Build your schedule by editing directly in the table or using the "Add Course" bar.

AUDIT VISUALIZATION (Instructor/TA Coloring):
• INSTRUCTOR NAMES – Turn GREEN when total theoretical teaching hours across all assigned courses is ≥6 for Sciences and ≥9 for Humanities . Turn RED when below 6 hours for Sciences and 9 for Humanities.
• TA NAMES – Turn GREEN when total practical hours ≥9. If a TA is assigned to a course WITHOUT an instructor, their theoretical + practical hours are summed and must be ≥9 to turn GREEN. Otherwise, RED.

CONFLICT DETECTION:
• Room & Time conflicts are automatically highlighted in red rows
• Instructor double-booking conflicts are flagged
• Co-requisite violations appear in orange rows (admin override available)

STUDENT VIEW: Toggle to "Student" mode to see which courses are offered versus missing for each class year (Freshman through Senior), based on prerequisite depth analysis.

Use column filters to search, click column headers to sort, publish completed semesters for future reuse.
